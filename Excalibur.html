Excalibur

This is Excalibur who is known for being a swordmaster and his abilities revolve around his personal sword the Exalted Blade. Excalibur is one of the three starting frames you can choose to start your journey with and is one of the more powerful frames. His abilities can make him do a sword dash at the enemies and there is a chance to chain to multiple enemies, he can blind all nearby enemies, he can also send daggers flying from his sword strike nearby enemies dealing damage, blinding them, and stunning them, and finaly he can pull out his sword the Exalted Blade and do new combos using his power combined with the blades unique style.
