Frost

This is Frost one of the better tanks that you can have on your side his ability centers around ice. His first ability is to shoot out a energy blast from his hand freezing all enemies in the path and making them freeze in their tracks, his second ability is to send a wave of razor sharp ice towards his enemies dealing heavy amounts of damage to the enemies caught in the wave, his third ability is a globe made of ice surround him and allies protecting them from outside damage from enemy fire and abilities, and his fourth and final ability is to summon a landslide of ice to freeze and shatter all enemies in the radius.
